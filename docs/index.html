A bit about me...

My name is Tonya Velcko. I am commonly known on various sites as LittleCactus, but that name was already taken on GitHub. When I realized this, I felt like I had lost a beloved possession, like a hat, for instance. thus, my new name was adopted: HatlessCactus!

I am currently a student at Penn State University, the Behrend College, majoring in Digital Media, Arts, and Technology with a minor in Game Development. I have three dogs, each roughly the size of a shoe box, and multiple plants. In my free time, I enjoy reading, writing, playing video games, creating chaos in my Dungeons and Dragons campaign, and spending time with family and friends!
